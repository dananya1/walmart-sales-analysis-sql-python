# Walmart Sales Analysis - Business Insights

## Project Overview
This project analyzes Walmart sales data using SQL and Python to understand customer behavior, sales performance, payment trends, and product category performance. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) was performed using pandas, matplotlib, and seaborn.

---

# Key Business Insights

## 1. Top Performing Cities
The sales analysis revealed that a few cities generated significantly higher total sales compared to others. These cities contribute a major portion of overall revenue and can be considered high-demand markets.

## 2. Customer Payment Preferences
The payment method distribution analysis showed that certain payment methods are more commonly used by customers. This insight helps businesses optimize payment options and improve customer convenience.

## 3. Product Category Distribution
Some product categories recorded higher transaction counts than others, indicating stronger customer demand and better product movement in those categories.

## 4. Customer Ratings Analysis
Most customer ratings were concentrated between 4 and 7, suggesting that the majority of customers had a moderate-to-good shopping experience.

## 5. Sales Drivers
A new feature called `total_sales` was created by multiplying unit price and quantity. Analysis showed that higher unit prices and larger quantities directly contributed to increased sales revenue.

## 6. Correlation Analysis
The correlation heatmap identified relationships between:
- Unit Price
- Quantity
- Ratings
- Profit Margin
- Total Sales

The analysis showed that total sales are strongly influenced by unit price and quantity purchased.

## 7. Data Quality Improvements
The dataset was cleaned by:
- Removing duplicate records
- Handling datatype issues
- Converting numeric columns properly for analysis

This improved the reliability of business insights generated from the dataset.

---

# Recommendations

- Focus marketing strategies on top-performing cities.
- Maintain inventory levels for high-demand categories.
- Promote frequently used payment methods for smoother transactions.
- Improve customer experience to increase ratings further.
- Use pricing and quantity analysis to optimize sales revenue.
